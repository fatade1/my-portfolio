About Me

Design is how I
solve problems.

I'm FFH — a Creative Director, Brand Designer, and Product Designer based in Ibadan, Nigeria. I lead brand strategy and digital product design for startups, tech companies, recruitment firms, fashion brands, analytics companies, and ICT businesses.

As the Managing Director of Cyber Haven Co-Workspace, I operate at the intersection of creativity, business strategy, and technology. My work spans complete brand identity systems, UI/UX for SaaS platforms and fintech products, and strategic content direction.

I don't just design — I build systems that help brands communicate with clarity, connect with their audience, and scale with confidence. Every pixel is intentional. Every brand decision is strategic.
