Arteesa

Arteesa is a digital platform designed to connect skilled artisans with clients who need their services. From carpenters and tailors to painters and technicians, the platform bridges the gap between talent and opportunity — making it easier for people to find trusted professionals and for artisans to get consistent work.

The Problem

In many parts of Nigeria, finding reliable artisans is still based on referrals, trial and error, or word of mouth.

Clients struggle with:

  • Trust and credibility
  • Lack of structure in pricing and communication
  • Difficulty finding the right artisan quickly

Artisans struggle with:

  • Visibility
  • Access to consistent jobs
  • Professional presentation

The Goal

To design a platform that:

  • Makes it easy to discover and hire artisans
  • Builds trust through structured profiles and reviews
  • Creates a professional digital presence for artisans
  • Simplifies communication and service requests

My Role

Product Designer (UI/UX) & Creative Direction
I was responsible for shaping the product experience from concept to design, ensuring usability, clarity, and scalability.

What I Did

🔍 Product Strategy & User Flow Design

  • Mapped out user journeys for both clients and artisans
  • Defined how users discover, select, and hire artisans
  • Structured the onboarding experience for both user types

📱 UI/UX Design

  • Designed clean, intuitive mobile and web interfaces
  • Focused on ease of navigation and accessibility
  • Built layouts that simplify decision-making for users

🧩 Feature Design

  • Artisan profiles (skills, ratings, portfolio, reviews)
  • Search & filtering system (location, service type, rating)
  • Booking/request flow
  • Messaging/communication interface

🎨 Design System

  • Established color palette, typography, and UI components
  • Ensured consistency across all screens
  • Designed scalable components for future expansion

The Approach

The design focused on trust, simplicity, and accessibility. Since the platform serves a wide range of users (including non-tech-savvy individuals), the experience was kept:

  • Simple to understand
  • Easy to navigate
  • Visually clean but functional

The goal was to reduce friction and make the process of hiring an artisan feel seamless and reliable.

The Outcome

Arteesa provides a structured, user-friendly solution that connects artisans to real opportunities, helps clients find trusted professionals faster, and introduces a more organized system into an informal market.

It transforms a traditionally offline, scattered process into a digital, scalable experience.

Key Deliverables
